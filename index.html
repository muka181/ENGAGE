Strengthening health systems

Machine learning can use health data to improve community health and inform efficient use of scarce resources by helping health programmes identify and prioritise interventions. Creating a diverse pool of trained data scientists in Kenya is an essential part of the solution for building health systems that can respond to both the needs of local communities and the country.
